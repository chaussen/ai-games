The hierarchy — one structure, every book

The Chapter (卷) is the configurable joint: it absorbs how each book groups lessons, so 中文 (3 lessons/unit) and Band 1 (flat units) share one levelling + award cadence.

Flat units, no 3-grouping. 卷 Chapter = a teacher-defined cluster — e.g. 2 units = 1 chapter, or 1 unit = 1 chapter. Same seal + rank fires.

Unit A · Unit Bcluster → 卷 → SEAL + RANK
Reward cadence is decoupled from “lessons per unit”: per-stage pays stars + 文 + XP every session; per-chapter pays the seal + a 文 bonus + the XP that usually tips a rank-up. The chapter hides the structural difference from the scoring engine.

THE MERGE · Unit gives the what + why; the graph gives the order within. Parts come first even when they aren’t “vocabulary”.

DECK · The collection meta-game AND the gate: own the parts → unlock the wholes. Shared across the whole book.

ANTI-FARM · Only due chars pay +8 文 — and the schedule decides due-ness, not the student. Re-grinding a cleared stage pays +1.

Decay gaps ≈ 1 / 3 / 7 / 21 days (Leitner). Refresh restores mastery + re-inks the scroll.

No decay of 文 or XP — only character ink fades. A nudge, never a penalty.

XP → 科举 rank

Stage clear +10 XP/char, plus stars ★+0 · ★★+5 · ★★★+15; seal +100 (usually tips the rank-up). Ladder: 学童→蒙生→书生→秀才→举人→贡士→进士→状元.

Mastery & review

Per char 0–3; decays on a schedule → ink fades → due. Due chars resurface as review checkpoints; refreshing pays +8 文 & restores mastery.

Owned parts

Stroke-forging a component marks it owned (shared book-wide). Gates composites; counted in the Deck, not the vocabulary ring.

Every worry → its lever:   weak → 文 pays completion not stars ·   unwilling → low everyday tier + beat-your-own-best ·   absent / home → no decay, floor, welcome-back ·   re-grinding → re-practice +1, only due pays ·   top students → XP / rank / prestige.

11

The 文 coin — visual brief

An ancient round cash-coin with a square hole (天圆地方). Reads instantly as money; the legend is 文 — a coin and the word for “a character / writing.” Pure geometry, no illustration.

owned · full coin
locked · outline
wallet · a stack

Glyph sits above the hole (as on real coins) so it stays legible down to the 18px header chip.

Warm metal: gold rim #E8B85A, darker face, square hole shows the paper through.

Motion: on a stage clear, coins fly from the reveal into the wallet and the count ticks.

Upgrade later: a seal-script 文, or a fully illustrated coin.

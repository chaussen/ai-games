### English (for parents)

Dear families,

The holidays are here! To keep your child happily connected to Chinese
characters at home, we're opening a **free holiday trial of The Character
Game — Book 1** for every family in the class.

- 📖 **What they'll play:** all four units of Book 1 (Spring · Summer · Autumn ·
  Winter). Children first watch each character paint itself stroke by stroke,
  then build it from its pieces, and finally use the characters to make words
  and sentences — like a level-by-level adventure.
- ✨ **Why kids love it:** colourful stroke-order animations, happy
  celebrations, and seals and ranks to collect. There's a simple guided tour,
  so children can start on their own.
- ⏰ **Trial length:** free for **14 days** per device.
- 📱 **How to start:** open the link on a phone or tablet — **no download, no
  sign-in, no cost**:
  **https://play.caseychinese.org/trial.html**

**A tip for parents:** 10–15 minutes a day is plenty. Let your child say the
sounds aloud and trace the strokes with a finger, and cheer with them when they
clear a level — it keeps them motivated. The full game (all four books) is
unlocked in class, so come back after the break to keep going.

Happy holidays — see you in class!

# 假期免费试用通知 · Holiday Free Trial Notice

A ready-to-send notice for parents about the Book 1 holiday free trial.
Copy/paste into WeChat, email, or a class group. The English and Chinese say
the same thing — use whichever fits your families (or send both).

**Trial link:** https://play.caseychinese.org/trial.html

---

- 🔗 **打开方式**：手机或平板点开链接即可，无需下载、无需登录：

## English (for parents)

Dear families,

The holidays are here! So your child can keep enjoying Chinese characters at
home, we've opened a **free holiday trial of The Character Game — Book 1**.

- 📖 **What's included:** all four units of Book 1 (Spring · Summer · Autumn ·
  Winter) — from strokes all the way to building words and sentences, in a
  fun level-by-level adventure.
- ⏰ **How long:** a **14-day** free trial per device.
- 🎒 **Full version:** all four books are unlocked in class — come along to keep
  playing and learning!
- 🔗 **How to start:** just open the link on a phone or tablet — no download,
  no sign-in needed:
  **https://play.caseychinese.org/trial.html**

Let your child play a little and write a little, and feel the joy of Chinese
characters. See you in class!

---

### Notes for the teacher (not for parents)

- The trial is **Book 1 only** and runs for **14 days per device**, tracked in the
  browser. It is a holiday taster, not a full account — clearing the browser
  storage resets the clock, which is fine for a trial.
- The class build is unchanged and still lives at
  https://play.caseychinese.org/ (class code as before). The trial is a
  separate page (`/trial.html`) with no class code and no teacher console.
- A real per-student account system with a true seat limit needs a backend —
  that's the planned next step.
